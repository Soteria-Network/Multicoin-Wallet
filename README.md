## What is Soteria Multicoin Wallet?

**Soteria Multicoin Wallet** is a secure, non‑custodial multi‑coin wallet that gives you full control of your private keys.  
It supports the vast majority of cryptocurrencies and comes with a built‑in decentralized exchange (AtomicDEX) for true peer‑to‑peer trading.  
Swap Soter and other assets directly from your wallet—no centralized exchange, no KYC, and no third‑party risk.  
With multiple wallet types to choose from, a polished desktop and mobile experience, and seamless cross‑chain interoperability, it’s the safest, all‑in‑one portal to manage, trade, and grow your crypto portfolio.

Soteria Multicoin Wallet has been tested on the following platforms:

- Windows 10, 11
- Linux (Ubuntu 22.04+)
- macOS (11.0+)
